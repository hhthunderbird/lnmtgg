Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

Welcome to Apex Racing, an online multiplayer racing game developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users:

  • Camera Access: We request access to your device’s camera solely for features such as in-game avatar customization, AR mode, or in-game streaming. No camera data is collected or stored unless explicitly initiated by the user.
  • Gameplay Data: Information such as username, player ID, race results, ranking, and customization settings.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language, and country.
  • Log Data: Crash reports and diagnostic logs to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game’s core functionality
  • To enable multiplayer features
  • To personalize user experience and save preferences
  • To improve game stability and performance
  • To respond to user support requests

3. Camera Permission

The camera is used only with your explicit permission. Apex Racing may use the camera for features such as:

  • Customizing your player avatar using AR or selfies
  • Augmented reality racing features (if enabled)
  • Capturing in-game moments (e.g., screenshots or clips)

We do not collect or transmit camera data to our servers unless you choose to share it (e.g., uploading a photo or recording).

4. Third-Party Services

Apex Racing may integrate with third-party services such as:

  • Photon Engine: For real-time multiplayer networking
  • Google Play Games: For leaderboard, achievements, and sign-in
  • Analytics Providers: For crash tracking and performance analysis (e.g., Firebase)

These third parties have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain your gameplay data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@apexracinggame.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Apex Racing is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the information we store about you
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw camera or other app permissions at any time via your device settings

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no digital transmission or storage system is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@apexracinggame.com
